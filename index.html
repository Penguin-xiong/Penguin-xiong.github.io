Thoughts Spilling onto the Web

I don't think that anyone on this Earth has ever had it easy, if so, they are probably not living life correctly. It took me a long time to build this mindset but... I am not going to quit, never, no, not ever! Before I leave this Earth, there is something that I still have to show the world. I have envisioned it perfectly in my mind, and if only I could turn you into a thought, you'd be a guess in my mind and see my life's dream. Maybe then you'd sail with me to this utopian land.

I wonder if it is true. That we are all made up of light, existing in different frequencies yet bound together in this physical world. Sometimes when I see people, plants, or things--maybe I am just staring for too long--but it's almost as if the air around them start to glow. It could be a trick with the fluorescent/sun, but then again, maybe we really are all made up of light. Maybe some moments are just meant to be ours and we shine, literally.
